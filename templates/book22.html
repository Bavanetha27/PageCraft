About

Engineering Chemistry: Concepts for Engineers has been designed in accordance with the new syllabus of various Indian universities. The book includes eleven chapters and fourteen experiments as per the latest syllabus for the engineering students. The book provides in depth knowledge on various topics in a very lucid manner. Many solved and unsolved university questions and intext questions for practice are included at the end of each chapter.
